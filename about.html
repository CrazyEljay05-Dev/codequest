About CodeQuest

CodeQuest is an interactive website built to make learning coding fun and accessible for students. Our goal is to turn complex programming concepts into engaging activities, challenges, and games that help learners explore coding step-by-step.

Whether you’re a beginner or just curious about programming, CodeQuest provides a simple and exciting journey to understand how coding works — from basic logic to creating your first projects.
